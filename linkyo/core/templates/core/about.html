About Linkyo


Linkyo is a link management application developed by our team.
It allows users to efficiently manage and organize custom links
for their business, social media, and other purposes.
The app offers features such as link organisation, grouping links
into categories, a user dashboard, and custom link creation.
Our aim is to provide users with a streamlined soultion for
managing their links effectively.
